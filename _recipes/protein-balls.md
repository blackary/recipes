---
layout: recipe
title: "Protein Balls"
photo: ""
ingredients:
  - "1 cup oats"
  - "1/2 cup peanut butter"
  - "1/2 cup chocolate chips"
  - "1/3 cup honey"
  - "1 tablespoon chia seeds"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "1/2 cup ground flaxseed (optional)"
instructions:
  - "Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl."
  - "Stir until fully blended and the mixture becomes sticky."
  - "Roll into 1–1.5 inch balls."
  - "Chill for 20–30 minutes to firm up."
  - "Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator."
servings: None
---

Great for quick snacks or lunch boxes. Adjust texture with extra oats (to firm) or extra peanut butter/honey (to loosen).
